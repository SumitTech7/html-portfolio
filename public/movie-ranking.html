The Best Movies According to Sumit

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Forrest Gump

‘Forrest Gump’ is a movie that follows the life events on an underrated and underappreciated man that suffers from not only a low IQ, but also mild case of cerebral palsy. Throughout his life, Gump faces many burdens and tribulations, but he never allows anyone to interfere with his happiness and mental well-being. Gump, despite facing many problems, continues to believe that good things will happen and goes after his hopes, dreams, and ambitions.

Interstellar

Interstellar combines realistic sci-fi, emotional depth, and breathtaking visuals, creating an awe-inspiring cosmic journey. The film's scientific accuracy appeals to enthusiasts while its exploration of the parent-child bond adds emotional weight. Christopher Nolan's direction immerses viewers in the grandeur of space, showcasing planets and phenomena with unparalleled beauty. The story's philosophical themes and mind-bending concepts invite contemplation. With an epic scale spanning galaxies, the stakes are high, keeping audiences engaged. Hans Zimmer's powerful score enhances the emotional resonance. These elements converge to make 'Interstellar' a cinematic masterpiece for those seeking a profound and visually stunning experience

Gravity

You likely appreciate "Gravity" for its stunning visuals, suspenseful plot, realistic portrayal of space, strong performances, and emotional resonance. The film's scientific accuracy, minimalist approach, and acclaimed status also contribute to its appeal.
